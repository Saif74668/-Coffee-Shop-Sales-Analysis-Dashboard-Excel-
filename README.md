# 📊 Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project presents a **Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and store-level business insights across multiple locations.

The dashboard provides an interactive and data-driven overview of key business metrics, helping stakeholders understand customer trends, product performance, and operational efficiency.

## Dashboard Features

* **Total Sales Analysis** – Overall revenue generated across stores
* **Customer Footfall Tracking** – Total number of customers served
* **Average Bill & Order Analysis** – Customer spending behavior insights
* **Hourly Sales Trends** – Identification of peak business hours
* **Category-wise Sales Distribution** – Revenue contribution by product categories
* **Top Selling Products** – Best-performing products based on sales
* **Store Location Performance** – Comparison of footfall and sales across stores
* **Order Size Distribution** – Analysis of beverage order sizes
* **Daily & Weekly Order Patterns** – Understanding customer activity trends

## Key Business Insights

* Peak sales activity occurs between **8 AM – 10 AM**, indicating strong morning demand.
* **Coffee products** generate the highest revenue contribution, followed by bakery items.
* **Barista Espresso** is the top-performing product by sales.
* **Astoria** and **Hell’s Kitchen** locations demonstrate higher customer footfall and revenue generation.
* **Regular** and **Large-sized beverages** account for the majority of customer orders.
